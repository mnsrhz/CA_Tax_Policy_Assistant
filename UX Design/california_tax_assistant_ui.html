Can I deduct my home office expenses if I'm a W-2 employee working remotely in California?
⚖
Answer based on 3 source documents · Tax year 2024

No — as a W-2 employee you cannot deduct home office expenses on either your federal or California state return for 2024.

Under the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act (TCJA), the federal deduction for unreimbursed employee business expenses — including home office — was suspended through 2025. California conforms to this federal change for W-2 employees, meaning the suspension applies on your CA Form 540 as well.

The home office deduction remains available only if you are self-employed and report income on Schedule C, or if you have net earnings from a partnership or S-corporation shown on Schedule E.
